About Me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of British Columbia (UBC), where my research is advised by Professor Colin Macdonald and Professor Bhushan Gopaluni. My work focuses on numerical methods and the application of mathematics in machine learning.

My passion for mathematics stems from its power to model the world and uncover hidden structures. I am proficient in Python (with libraries like PyTorch) and Matlab. I'm always eager to apply my analytical skills to solve challenging problems, whether in an academic or industry setting.
